About & Resume

Hello and welcome to my portfolio!

I am an entry-level game developer with over 15 years of hobbyist game development experience. I have devoted the last four years of my life to attending college in pursuit of turning my love and passion for creating things into a career. I have a strong fondness for mathematics, including linear algebra and calculus, and I also enjoy participating in game jams with my classmates and friends.

My primary focus is on graphics and gameplay programming in C++, whether it's with my own engine or Unreal Engine. Additionally, I have a deep appreciation for working in Unity using C#. My journey began with asset creation at a very young age when I received my very first computer, and I have since honed my skills in modeling, texturing, rigging, animation, and all related aspects of game development.
